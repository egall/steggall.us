The Steggall Family

According to www.stagles.co.uk we are the descendents of "people who lived near the steep ascent". This is the favored theory as Steggalls are know for loving both ascending and descending steep slopes, be it on a bike, skateboard, snowboard or skiis. Steggalls are known for their love for athletics, academics, and the arts. All Steggalls are reffered to as "Steggs", which can cause confusion when there are multiple Steggalls an area.

Coconut "Chickin" Steggall

Coconut, aka "The chicken" is a cat of refined taste. She spends her time sleeping and grooming her beautiful coat. Chicken is known for being skiddish and scampering away at the first sign of a disturbance.

Pippy "Pit" Steggall

Pippy, aka "P-bird" is a young bird who enjoys hanging out with her best friend Gerdy. Her favorite activities include sleeping, walking around on the floor, and pooing on things.

Monkey "Chunks" Steggall

Monkey is a kitten who never grew up, her favorite hobbies are sleeping, eating, and chasing things. Monkey is an avid hunter and will frequently capture small animals animals and release them in the house. Her nicknames include, but are not limited to; Chunks, Smurfson, Murff, Chunkasaurous Rex, Lady Chunksfield, Ms Murffinson, Monkey-paco-the-chunky-taco, The Chunky Chalupa, and Munks.

Gerdy "The Elephant" Steggall

Though he may not be a large animal he certainly can make his presence felt by sheer ferocity. Gertby is known for attacking humans 100x his size with reckless abandon. He also enjoys making a mess with his food and hanging out with Pippy. Gerdy is also known as; Gertby, Snerd, Herbert Hoover, Herb, Snerdby, Gert, and Herdby.
